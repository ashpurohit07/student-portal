About

Built for students, by learners

Smart Learning Hub was created to solve one problem: scattered, hard-to-find academic resources. We believe every student deserves organized, quick access to the materials they need — semester by semester, subject by subject.

The platform supports both students and admins with role-based dashboards, upload workflows, and a clean interface that gets out of your way.
